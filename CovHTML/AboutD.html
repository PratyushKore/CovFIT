About CovFIT

CovFIT is a software created to detect masks and help spread the importance of masks. It is important to ensure everyone is wearing their masks, especially in places like classrooms and large gatherings. CovFIT makes sure each person is wearing their mask as they enter. CovFIT also makes it easy for moderators to see who is and isn't wearing their mask.
